My process begins with **Empathy & Research** to understand user workflows, followed by **Information Architecture & Wireframing** to map interactions. Next, I design **High-Fidelity UI elements** in Figma, and complete the loop with **Interactive Prototyping** and usability validation.

I specialize in **Figma** for comprehensive UI designs and interactive prototypes, **Blender** for isometric 3D asset modeling and lighting, **Adobe Illustrator** for vector artwork, and **Adobe XD** / **Canva** for user flows and promotional branding kits.

As a Bachelor of Engineering graduate, I have built strong logical and analytical foundations. This helps me collaborate closely with developers, align layouts with technical grids, write tidy documentation, and build UI designs that are both functional and scalable to implement.
